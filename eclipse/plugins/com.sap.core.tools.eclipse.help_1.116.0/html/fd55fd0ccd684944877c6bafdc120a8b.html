Content Services

Content services are based on the HCP document service, which enables you to store your content with the option of using SAP Mobile Documents to share content securely with your business partners.

The HCP document service is already available as the content persistence for new applications that you build. It is easy to consume, highly scalable, and secure. In addition, it is based on the Content Management Interoperability Services (CMIS) open standard. The document service supports the following document management capabilities:
  • All document and file types
  • Folder hierarchies
  • Property models
  • Access control
  • Versioning
SAP Mobile Documents provides a ready-to-use solution for sharing content based on the SAP Cloud Platform, as well as an extension platform to integrate custom repositories and custom clients. In addition, it can be integrated as a tile into the SAP Fiori launchpad. This way, SAP Mobile Documents enables access to existing on-premise business content, for example, documents that are stored in Microsoft SharePoint or SAP Business Suite. Users can share content to collaborate with their business partners in a compliant way. Moreover, business document templates and standards are available company-wide.

On top of the ready-to-use solution, you can use SAP Mobile Documents to integrate a sharing functionality into your existing applications, implement your own clients for advanced scenarios, and extend ABAP connectivity to support your business processes.
