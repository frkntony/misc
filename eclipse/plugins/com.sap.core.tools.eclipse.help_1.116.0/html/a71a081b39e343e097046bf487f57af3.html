Purchasing a Customer Account

A customer account allows you to host productive, business-critical applications with 24x7 support.

When you want to purchase a customer account, you can select from a set of predefined packages. For more information, see https://hcp.sap.com/pricing.html. Contact us on SAP Cloud Platform or via an SAP sales representative.

In addition, you can upgrade and refine your resources later on. You can also contact your SAP sales representative and opt for a configuration, tailored to your needs.

After you have purchased your customer account, you will receive an e-mail with a link to the landing page of SAP Cloud Platform.
