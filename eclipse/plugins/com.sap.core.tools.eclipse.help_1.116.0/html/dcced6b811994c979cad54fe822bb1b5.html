Assign Gamification Roles

Prerequisites

You have logged on to the SAP Cloud Platform cockpit with your SCN user and password.

Procedure

  1. In the SAP Cloud Platform cockpit, choose the Services tab.
  2. Click the Gamification Service tile.
  3. Click on the Configure Gamification Service link.

  4. Go to the Roles tab.
  5. Assign the necessary roles, for example AppStandard, AppAdmin, GamificationDesigner, TenantOperator, and helpdesk.
