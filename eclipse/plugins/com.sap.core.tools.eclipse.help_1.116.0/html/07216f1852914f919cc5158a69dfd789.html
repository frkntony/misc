Managing Repositories in the Cockpit

In the cockpit, you can create, edit, and delete a document service repository for your accounts. In addition, you can monitor the number and size of the tenant repositories of your document service repository.

Note

Due to the tenant isolation in SAP Cloud Platform, the document service cockpit cannot access or view repostories you create in SAP Document Center or vice versa.
